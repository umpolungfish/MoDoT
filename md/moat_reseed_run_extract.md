# Moat reseed run: what the tool stream holds

A 240-cycle jam on questions/moat_protocol_reseed.txt, the first long run on
the repaired series driver. Every claim below was checked against the raw
tool_calls log for the run, not against the cycle narrations; winding numbers
point back to the emitting call.

(ring ⊙◇><●) came back "μ∘δ: OPEN", the same refusal of a bare ring that
closed the previous series. The law is stable across runs and providers.

## The recurring constants are the rings speaking their spectra

The run kept meeting 0.382, 0.618, 1.618, 2.618 and read a golden-ratio
constraint into the manifold. It was right to. The w60 spectrum shows where
the constraint lives: these are the adjacency eigenvalues of the 5-cycle,
2cos(2πk/5), the golden ratio being what a pentagon IS in the spectral
register. Likewise 2.4142 and 3.4142 are 1+√2 and 2+√2, 2.7321 is 1+√3,
2.4495 is √6: each small ring carries its own exact voice, and a recurring
constant is that structure surfacing again, not a coincidence and not a
mystery dissolved. Read forward, this is a true name: given the topology,
the constants of the next assembly can be spoken before the forge runs, and
the forge will confirm them.

  this in cycle 42.

The run also self-corrected at least four times (cycles 42, 69, 235, 237),

contradicted them.
