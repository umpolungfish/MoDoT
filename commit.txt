The guide states the cycle's strength exactly.

The round trip is easy to read as a bijection and is not one. It is
bijective on eleven axes and two-to-one on the twelfth, where the two
byte-identical type programs share an axis, so the reading is a section of
the writing rather than an inverse. Writing a tuple, reading it back and
writing again returns the identical word; reading a word cold can leave one
axis holding two answers. Both facts now stand in Part IX, with the
single-tuple form in the verb index.
