I split the parrot guard's thresholds so fresh guesses stop dying as parrots.

The single 3/5 word-overlap threshold, applied against every taken guess, was
refusing genuinely new excriptions the moment two short guesses shared a pair
of words ("sailing route planner" vs "railway route planner"), and candidates
starved through both attempts. Now the spent examples keep the strict 3/5
reading (the example resurfacing is radioactive at any phrasing), while taken
guesses only refuse a NEAR-DUPLICATE at 7/10 overlap, checked against the
most recent sixty: distinct objects in one domain legitimately share
vocabulary.

Verified: build green under local,cuda; 5/5 learn tests; a live tri round of
five candidates produced zero parrot rejections, and the seed's accuracy
trajectory across runs reads 14.9 then 11.0 then 7.75 with six hundred three
words visited: the curve the loop exists to bend is bending.
