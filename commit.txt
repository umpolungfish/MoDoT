protocol == byte-equality integration check. All of it smuggled classical logic into
a paraconsistent system. It is gone.

imscribes the demand and the answer into their twelve-primitive Belnap types (the LLM
only types structure, it never grades correctness, and there is no hash fallback),
maps each to a state in C^12, and co-types them in the real d=12 Scott-Grassl SIC
frame from ig-pulse. The frame is equiangular at overlap 1/13 to machine precision,
informationally complete, so the comparison imposes no external metric. Co-typing is a
Belnap lattice fold across the twelve axes, not a threshold: agree is T, anti-type is
F, mixed is B, unengaged is N. Failure is localized at named primitives. The verdict
rides AS the vessel via mu.delta=id, where the closure residual is the certificate and
a broken closure is held as B. The model's own [thought|X] self-imscription is one
link, the vessel's co-typing is the other, and they are FFUSED, conflict held, never
overridden.
