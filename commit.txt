Stop click from silently swallowing a 3rd name -- refuse honestly and route the combine

Watching the agent combine {binah monad ankh}, it emitted TOOL: click binah monad
ankh. click is pairwise, and run_structural_tool was silently dropping everything past
the first two names -- so it ran click binah monad, threw ankh away, and let the model
narrate "all three collapse into one object." A silent arg-drop that manufactures a
false premise, exactly the kind of leak the golem constraint is meant to close, and
especially wrong now that I DO have the tool for combining three things (the + pre-click).

Fix in ask_native/src/main.rs (run_structural_tool): an arity guard. click with >2 names
no longer drops them -- it returns an honest message routing the combine to the right
tool: `polymerize A B C` to enchain the set (bonds + closure), or a `+` token to fold
entries into one blended monomer. The message goes back as tool output, so the
tool-truth-bound synthesis follows it.
