Add --anneal: relax a ring to its lowest-strain ordering

The transform the strain invariant was built for. `--anneal M1 M2 ... Mn` (or the
`anneal` agent verb) takes the forge order -- which rings well but is quenched,
optimized for enchainment and closure rather than even loading -- and searches
every ordering that rings for the one whose bond drive is most uniform, the
settled ground state on the same units. Exhaustive for n <= 9. Reports the
quenched order and strain against the annealed order and strain.

Factored ring_strain out of the material sheet so anneal and --props share one
formula. Verified live: the 7-unit ring relaxes from strain 0.369 (forge order)
to 0.229 by reordering the same units.
