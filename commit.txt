The trilattice oracle is CLI-reachable: --imasm 16_3 check <glyph word>

The classic eval verbs silently drop tokens they do not know, so a tri-word
labeled through them gets a label for a different, filtered word. The 16_3
register machine now answers through the imasm dispatch, so external
labelers reach the real trilattice verdict space (T/N/B/F) and sink.
