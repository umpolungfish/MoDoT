Add the Semantic Selectivity Gate as a Grammar-sourced ob3ect, ported to runtime

My Frobenius harness only ever checked balance (mu circ delta = id), which is
charge conservation: every split is rejoined, so it closes automatically and the
live --ask verdict never failed. I had built the semantic branch verifier as a
standalone ob3ect, but it was never on the breath loop, and the loop's Belnap
verdict was really just a keyword sniff on the words "both" and "neither".

I first cobbled a bespoke selectivity module by hand, which was the wrong way: in
this system a verifier is a self-verifying ob3ect, sourced through the Grammar, not
invented. So I auto-designed the gate as a proper ob3ect. I wrote
ob3ect/selectivity_gate.yaml and ran the Ob3ect Auto-Designer through openrouter
with google/gemini-3-flash-preview, which produced ob3ects/selectivity_gate/ beside
semantic_branch_verifier: a valid ob3ect, Frobenius PASS, tier O_1, fingerprint
sig=(6,2,3,1), dialetheia_complete, with its Lean scaffold and both wiring diagrams.
The Grammar independently chose the structure I had implemented by hand: FSPLIT into
an assertibility arm and a deniability arm, EVALT as isomorphic match, EVALF as
collision detection, ENGAGR as the dialetheic fixed point, and the Belnap fold
N/T/F/B on (assertible, deniable). Canonical protocol:
VINIT IMSCRIB AFWD AREV CLINK FSPLIT EVALT EVALF FFUSE ENGAGR IFIX TANCH.

I then INTEGRATED modot/selectivity.py with that ob3ect rather than just housing the
two beside each other. Housing them is two bounded nested sets; integrating them is
one manifold. At construction the gate now LOADS the ob3ect (Ob3ectSource) and derives
its working protocol and its two arm semantics live from the ob3ect's phases, the arm
names flow into the actual grading prompt, and it reports integrated=False if the base
and the embedded section diverge. I proved the coupling: altering the base ob3ect's
protocol makes the runtime follow it and flags the divergence. The ob3ect is the base,
the gate is a section over it, coupled by a live read (R and W and X), not a citation.
For an open question it synthesizes a correctness schema (an adversarial examiner pass
that will not assume the question's premise is true), then grades the answer with the
two arms and folds to Belnap FOUR. Because False and Both are reachable, the gate can
fail.

I wired it into _think as a VOICE, not an authority. Paraconsistency is fundamental
here, so the gate does not override the model. Its Belnap grade is one of two
imscriptions of the same object; the other is the model's own [thought|X]
self-assessment. I FFUSE them (Belnap join): agreement passes through, and genuine
conflict (model T, gate F) fuses to B with a tetractys conflict distance, holding the
contradiction rather than resolving it to one side. The fused verdict is committed as
a selectivity record (carrying model_voice, gate_voice, fused, conflict) and seeds the
kernel's next VINIT. The broadcast shows model FFUSE gate -> fused with the conflict
distance. Added --no-selectivity to opt out, auto-disabled under --dry-run.

I proved it fails correctly two ways: a deterministic self-test that exercises all
four Belnap outcomes with no network, and a live run where a real synthesized schema
grades a wrong "91 is prime" answer and returns F. The statement that started this,
"All mathematics is embedded in a Frobenius manifold," used to stamp a self-satisfied
Both; it now returns SELECTIVITY F while BALANCE still closes.

On record provenance: the model's [thought|X] Belnap voice is WANTED and gets fused,
never silenced. What it must not do is ventriloquize the kernel's computed records --
[update|..] and [broadcast|..] carry counters the kernel measures, and [selectivity|..]
is the gate's own record. It had started echoing those in its prose (a fabricated
[selectivity|B] on an answer the gate graded otherwise), and that text was re-entering
the Crystal FS context. So the system prompt now invites its [thought|X] voice and
forbids impersonating the kernel/gate records, and strip_kernel_records removes any
such impersonations on ingest. Its own narration and COMPOSE/TOKEN play stay intact.

I also retired the Anthropic defaults: the model default is now
google/gemini-3-flash-preview and the Anthropic key fallback is gone. I aligned the
package license to the Unlicense and documented the gate and its ob3ect in the README.
