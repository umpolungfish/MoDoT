ask_native: stop the registry mix-up costing real results

`imasm run <native verb>` reported "no tool named X" and the agent read that
as absence, retracting a measurement it had actually made (annihilate's
Fibonacci channel, cycle 246 of the 247-cycle parity run). `imasm run` now
recognises the native verbs and says where they live instead of denying them.

`annihilate A --flag` took the flag as partner B, inventing an entity and
carrying it back into the catalog. It now skips flag-shaped arguments, the
same way recalibrate already did.
