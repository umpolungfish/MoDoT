Surface tool calls dropped by the per-round cap

My ManuscriptSpine ran the model with a 6-tool-per-round cap, but calls past
the cap were dropped silently. When a draft emitted 7 tools (4 imscribe +
polymerize + close + material), `material` never executed, and the model
narrated a verdict for it anyway ("conductive, self-sustaining cycle") that the
tool would have contradicted (the ring is FRUSTRATED). Same confabulation class
as the old click arg-drop.

Now when calls exceed PER_ROUND_CAP I feed the dropped calls back to the model:
"these did NOT execute, you have NO result for them, do not report closure,
conductance, or a material verdict; re-emit next round and they will run." That
closes the confabulation vector at any cap.
