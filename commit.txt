Chain the loops into a metabolism -- the pathway, a loop of loops that closes as a metabolic cycle

One cycle turns over one substrate. A pathway chains them.

--pathway SUBSTRATE C1 C2 ... Cn (run_pathway in ask_native/src/click.rs): the
substrate runs through the catalyst sequence, one turnover each, the product of
each step the substrate of the next. The winding quantum Ω is the CARRIER, passed
hand to hand down the chain -- the electron-transport / cofactor carrier of real
metabolism. Each step reuses the cycle logic (transfer_electron, reductive first
else oxidative); each catalyst is a fixed point that regenerates.

The payoff is closure. If the carrier RETURNS to its start (net delta-Omega = 0,
Solve and Coagula balanced across the whole chain), the pathway CLOSES into a
metabolic cycle: a loop of loops, mu-delta = id at the pathway level, the TCA
archetype, O∞. Otherwise it is an open pathway (carrier advanced) and the tool
says what would close it (a return leg, opposite-direction turnover).

--certify verifies each unique catalyst regenerates (certify_switch, deduped)
through lake build. Honest guards: a step where no carrier can pass reports the
pathway stalled, not a fake turnover.
