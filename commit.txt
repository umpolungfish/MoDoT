Fix imscribe case-mismatch and cap calls per round

catalog_has_name is now case-insensitive (via catalog_names), so imscribe
no longer reports "did not register" for a name the generator wrote in a
different case. And the ACT loop caps calls at 24 per round, running the
first 24 and deferring the rest, so no single round can flood (the 87-call
ladder round could not recur).
