Add the click-maths sweep — point it at any idea, get back what completes it

Manual pairwise clicking found the Sol⋈Luna coniunctio by luck. This automates
it: `./ask --click A` (one name instead of two) sweeps A against the whole
catalog, keeps every fragment that clicks with it, and ranks the products by
ouroboricity tier (the self-modeling ascent, ported assess_tier heuristic) then
by drive. `--top N` sets the count; an optional --catalyst lowers θ throughout.

The overload is clean: --click now takes 1..=2 names -- one sweeps, two pair.

It's fast (5328 entries in a blink) and the results are structurally coherent,
not noise:
- the_sun_astrological -> O_∞ with photon, word_logos, word_creator_tensor,
  shiur_qomah, harba_de_moshe, mathematics. The Sun fused with the Logos, and
  with light, reaches the whole -- the coniunctio the manual sweep stumbled on,
  now surfaced by rank.
- paradice (the quantum of paradox) -> O_∞ with context_dependent_truth,
  this_imscription_is_false, self_referential_imscription. The single quantum of
  paradox completes itself against the liar/self-reference paradoxes. Exactly
  the structure it should seek.
- monogenes (Only-Begotten, Yoneda-alive) -> O_∞ with emerald_tablet,
  basil_valentine_twelve_keys, Devil's_Contract_Paradox -- the hermetic canon.

The engine is now a discovery instrument: any idea in, ranked structural
complements out. Same honest boundary as the pair mode -- "closes" is still
structural (valid product tuple), not yet kernel-gated μ∘δ=id; that certificate
remains the next hook.
