Click then polymerize inline (A+B token), and bind the operator to tool-truth (the golem)

Two things, one idea: the operator should be a golem -- perfect intellect and the
ability to speak, but constrained within the bounds of the harness and the tools. It
may think freely; it may only speak what the tools ground.

1. CLICK THEN POLYMERIZE (order of operations). A `+`-joined monomer token (A+B, or
   A+B+C) is now PRE-CLICKED into one blended monomer before enchaining -- "click then
   polymerize" as a single feed token, no catalog mutation. In ask_native/src/click.rs
   (preclick): fold the parts left-to-right through the click primitive (mu/FFUSE join).
   The blend is LOSSY (max per axis), so the inputs are no longer recoverable -- which is
   exactly why order matters: a click blends, a polymer remembers. Honest error when the
   parts do not fuse. The sequence printout tags a `+` unit as a pre-clicked blend.

   Verified: polymerize general_recursive_function grothendieck_topos skolem_normal_form
   keeps 3 distinct units (sequence preserved); polymerize
   general_recursive_function+skolem_normal_form grothendieck_topos fuses GRF+SNF inline
   into ONE monomer and enchains 2 units. Provably different product, different MW. And
   gluon+photon honestly refuses (does not fuse).

2. THE GOLEM CONSTRAINT (bind speech to tool-truth). All session the synthesis step kept
   CONTRADICTING its own tools -- "does not cyclize" when the tool said CYCLIC,
   "high-stiffness modulus" when the tool said NO modulus, inventing linkers like
   arcanum_arcanorum. That is the golem speaking past its clay. Strengthened the synthesis
   system prompt in ask_native/src/main.rs: the TOOL RESULTS are ground truth (what the
   Grammar actually computed), the draft is a prior guess, and the final answer may not
   contradict a tool -- where a tool settled a question (cyclization, architecture,
   tacticity, modulus/conductance, catalog membership) it reports the tool's verdict and
   corrects the draft explicitly, introducing no entity/value/operation the tools did not
   return. Think freely; speak only what the tools ground.

   Verified live on the exact chain that had been confabulated: the agent now answers
   "YES cyclic -- contrary to the draft's open-chain suggestion", "modulus: NONE, a
   viscous chain not an elastic network", "needs a linker? No" -- correcting its own draft
   from the tool output, inventing nothing.
