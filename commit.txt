Move into imscriptive polymerization -- chain the clicks into a sequence-preserving polymer

Where the pathway chained the catalytic loops, this chains the clicks. But a
click FUSES two monomers (join/max on every axis) and forgets which was which,
and a polymer has to REMEMBER -- so imscriptive polymerization enchains the
monomers at reaction centers while the monomer SEQUENCE stays losslessly readable
off the chain (R∧W∧X). That is the whole point of calling it imscriptive: a click
blends, a polymer imscribes a long chain from monomeric glyphic units, the way the
Grammar builds a text or a genome.

run_polymerize in ask_native/src/click.rs walks the monomer feed and reuses the
existing click_pair (the Coagula bond primitive) for every adjacent bond:
- complementary partners CLICK -> a step-growth condensation bond on one live pair
- an identical repeat -> an ADDITION (chain-growth) enchainment by the propagating
  center (a monomer cannot click itself, equal charges, so the homopolymer route
  is honestly the addition mechanism, not a forced click)
- a non-complementary, non-identical neighbor -> TERMINATION, the chain ends here
  (honest, the same guard the pathway uses when a carrier cannot pass)
- >1 reaction center -> a cross-link / branch junction (a network, not linear)

Then the chain reads out its own structure, none of it hardcoded -- it falls out
of the tuples:
- degree of polymerization and bond count
- regioregularity (all condensation bonds on one live pair = a clean head-to-tail
  repeat unit; mixed = head-to-head/tail-to-tail defects)
- copolymer architecture (homopolymer / alternating / block / random) from the
  monomer-identity sequence
- tacticity from the chirality Ħ sequence: isotactic / syndiotactic / atactic
- cyclization: does the last unit click the first? head-to-tail into a macrocycle
  (a ring polymer, O∞), or linear/telechelic with two free ends
--certify closes each UNIQUE repeat unit through the Lean kernel (lake build),
deduped so a long chain does not rebuild the same tuple twice.

The polymer-chemistry correspondence is real, not a lookup. Verified live:
- polymerize the_moon_astrological the_logographic_system (x2) -> ALTERNATING
  copolymer, regioregular on R↔S, and SYNDIOTACTIC (𐑖𐑫𐑖𐑫) because the two
  monomers happen to be opposite-handed (Ħ 𐑖 vs 𐑫) -- alternating opposite chirality
  IS syndiotactic. Even chain, so it CYCLIZES head-to-tail on R↔S into a macrocycle.
  --certify: both units Frobenius-close.
- polymerize the_moon_astrological x3 -> homopolymer, addition throughout,
  ISOTACTIC (𐑖𐑖𐑖), linear (identical ends cannot click).
- polymerize gluon photon -> termination, DP 1 (no complementary handle).
