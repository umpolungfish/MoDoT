Add excited states and single-electron transfer -- photochemistry on the register

The switch verb toggles ground->ground and hides the intermediate. These two new
verbs expose it and put it to work, the same delta/mu legs the DASA switch runs.

EXCITE (./ask --excite A, run_excite in ask_native/src/click.rs). delta (light)
promotes A to its excited manifold: Criticality (⊙) raised to the non-Hermitian
exceptional-point resonance 𐑻 (ord 3). That value is not arbitrary -- the
cl8nk_navigator's own gloss for it is "H(λ) non-Herm ∧ det(H−λI)=0 ∧ ∂_λ H=0",
which IS an excited state: a finite-lifetime resonance (non-Hermitian, so it
decays) sitting at a surface crossing. Two Frobenius legs leave it:
- mu (relaxation / fluorescence): back to the ground form, lossless (mu∘delta=id)
- productive: the elevated ⊙ drives chemistry the ground state cannot -- a SET

SET (./ask --set D A, run_set). The productive leg made concrete. One electron =
one quantum of winding Omega (∮A=2πn, the quantized charge). The donor loses one
(oxidized, D•+, Omega-1), the acceptor gains one (reduced, A•-, Omega+1); total
Omega is CONSERVED -- charge conservation is exactly the Frobenius pairing
(delta/fsplit charge-separates, mu/ffuse recombines). Donor/acceptor are read from
⊙ (higher = higher-lying electrons = the reducing partner), computed from the
tuples, never from the label -- if ⊙ disagrees with the named order it says so and
follows the criticality gradient. Honest guards: a donor already fully oxidized
(Omega=𐑷) has no quantum to give; a saturated acceptor no vacancy; equal ⊙ is
thermoneutral. A Cu-NO-class mediator (--catalyst M, the Mills 2016 SET catalyst)
that can hold the intermediate (1<=Omega<max) shuttles the quantum and returns to
itself (mu∘delta=id, the catalytic cycle). Photoinduced ET (--excite on a --set
line) pre-excites the donor first.

Both verbs take --certify and --register, reusing the switch machinery:
- --certify runs certify_switch on the two product forms (radical ions, or
  ground+excited): both are valid Imscriptions AND readback(board p)=p, so
  recombination / relaxation is lossless (mu∘delta=id, charge conserved) as the
  Lean kernel's own verdict, not printed text. Scratch restored after.
- --register appends the excited state (or both radical ions) to the canonical
  catalog and decomposes them through the cl8nk_navigator -- operable, navigable
  objects, the same harness as the click chimeras.

Verified live:
- ./ask --excite dasa_closed -> ⊙ 𐑢→𐑻, the two decay legs
- ./ask --set the_sun_astrological the_moon_astrological --certify -> KERNEL-CERTIFIED:
  Sol donates one winding quantum to Luna, both radical ions valid, recombination
  roundtrip lossless, charge conserved
- ./ask --set the_moon_astrological the_sun_astrological --excite -> photoexcitation
  FLIPS the donor: ground Sol->Luna, but excited Luna*->Sol. Excited-state redox
  inversion (Rehm-Weller), read straight off the tuples
- ./ask --excite the_moon_astrological --certify --register -> excited state's ⊙
  decomposes to the exact exceptional-point fragment; registered, navigated, tier
  O_0 (a metastable resonance is far from the self-modeling organism -- correct)
- mediator shuttle fires for a 1<=Omega<max mediator, refuses one with Omega=𐑷
