The cycle primitives to imasm and back, and where it stops being invertible.

The forward leg was always here: a type is a full IMASM program, so a
twelve-axis tuple writes a word by concatenating its types in canonical
order. The return leg did not exist. It does now, and running both against
each other over the live catalog answers what the round trip actually
costs.

Three things it establishes. Type programs are NOT self-delimiting: the
type 'out' carries TANCH in its middle and does not end on the boundary
pair, so a concatenated word cannot be cut on the boundary and must be
parsed axis by axis, each axis consuming an admissible type's program as a
prefix. Position is load-bearing: the forty-nine types emit only
forty-seven distinct programs, byte-identical in sequence, topology,
spectral radius and closure, and of the two colliding pairs one is
separated by the axes it may occupy while the other shares an axis and
cannot be. At catalog scale no entry loses its original type, wherever the
reading is ambiguous the truth is among the pre-images, and the ambiguity
is confined to exactly the one axis the alphabet analysis predicts.

The one entry that breaks is a data defect, not a cycle defect: it carries
an axis value that is not one of the forty-nine glyphs, so the forward leg
has nothing to expand. Refusing it is correct; the entry wants repair.

The ambiguity is reported, not patched. Either the two types differ in
something the opcode composition does not carry, or the alphabet carries a
distinction without a difference. The cycle cannot settle which and does
not pretend to: it names both pre-images and the axis.

Also here, from the same run of work: the promotion path crosses faces
instead of silently truncating one endpoint, reads its verdict direction
off the endpoints rather than calling any change a climb, and gained a
churn that round-trips every waypoint through the learning loop; the loop
and the path now drive one shared round trip rather than two copies.
