Add ring strain and graph energy to the material sheet

Two more invariants off the roadmap ob3ects, both pure scalar reads (the ob3ects
typed them as reads, not transforms), so they live in the property sheet rather
than as their own verbs -- every --props / --forge / material sheet now carries
them.

  ring strain sigma(Delta): the population spread of clean-bond drive around the
    loop. Near zero when every junction carries equal tension, large when the
    ring is forced shut against reluctant bonds. This is the quantity the coming
    `anneal` reorder will drive down.

  graph energy sum|lambda|: the ring's total absolute spectral weight. Where the
    spectral radius rho is the ring's stiffness (its single dominant mode), this
    is its toughness -- the reserve carried across all the modes.

Verified against hand math: the pure 4-ring gives energy 4.0000 (|2|+0+0+|-2|),
the branched 3-ring gives 5.4641 (2.732+0.732+2.0).
