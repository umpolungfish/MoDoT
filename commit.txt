Add the purification/diagnostic bench: fpt, trap, stain — alchemical set complete

The last alchemical family, and with it all eleven classical techniques
from the ob3ect roadmap (alchemical_tools_roadmap) are in:
  - fpt M1 M2...       freeze-pump-thaw degassing: keep the units that form
    a real bond (drive >= theta) with at least one neighbor (the strongly-
    held core, EVALT), shed the ones that bond with nothing (the weakly-
    held filtrate, EVALF); a unit held only just at threshold is
    metastable (B). Runs on bond_forms/theta directly.
  - trap A [X]         ionic trapping: sequester A by its R<->S charge in a
    potential well (EVALT trapped) vs near-neutral tunneling escape
    (EVALF); a counter-partner X of opposite charge deepens the well, and
    the barrier lip is resonance (B). A held charge state, distinct from
    set (which moves an electron).
  - stain R M1 M2...   a family of diagnostic reagents keyed to a primitive
    (kmno4/uv -> Criticality, chiral -> Chirality, ninhydrin -> Recognition,
    iodine -> any live center): the units carrying the feature light up
    (EVALT revealed), the rest stay dark (EVALF), a borderline unit is a
    partial match (B).
