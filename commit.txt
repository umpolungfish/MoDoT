local ctx cap is measured, not guessed: with the weights resident, read the card's actual free VRAM (nvidia-smi, same pinned PCI ordinal) and this model's exact per-token KV cost from config.json, subtract headroom for prefill activations and a generation reserve, and that quotient is the prompt cap — a 4B on a 12 GB card lands ~10-14k instead of a hopeful 32k that OOMed the jam mid-series. MODOT_LOCAL_CTX still overrides; no flash-attn or no CUDA keeps the conservative 9000. pick_device now also reports which ordinal it actually landed on, and the load banner prints the real device instead of the configured wish.

calc's unexpected-character error now teaches instead of just refusing: it says calc is plain numeric and shows the model how to restate a bra-ket as numbers, so a jam agent that emits ⟨v_demand, v_answer⟩² gets steered to a computable expression instead of looping on the same glyph.
