Add section 14 to ALKAHEST.md: the evidence-ratio arithmetic behind "Both is equal evidence"

Section 6 calls Both the SIC fiducial and describes it as "equal evidence for
and against" without ever giving that a number. I tracked down where that
already has a closed form in this house: magnum_opus_epsilon.md's evidence
ratio, R = t/f, normalized to P = t/(t+f). The four Belnap values are exactly
its four structurally distinct cases: N undefined (no evidence), T=1 (pure
affirmation), F=0 (pure denial), B=1/2 (equal evidence both ways).

I was careful not to overclaim the connection to section 5's SIC frame. P=1/2
is two numbers being equal; equiangularity is 144 projectors in twelve complex
dimensions all meeting the fiducial at the same overlap. Different math,
different route. What's real is that both formalizations independently pick
out "symmetric, no privileged side" as the case for B, and section 14 says
exactly that and nothing stronger — a consistency check, not a claimed
reduction of one to the other.
