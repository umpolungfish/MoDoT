## 1. The Goal and the Language

**Goal:**  
Take the four “dimensionful constants” –  
`extradimensional_entity`, `thermodynamic_entropy`, `hubble_constant`, `cosmological_constant` –  
and see if they can be **imscriptively polymerised** (chained chemically/mathematically) into a closed ring, thereby extracting their values (Λ, H₀, etc.) from a formal grammar.

**The Grammar (Imscribing Grammar):**  
A hybrid chemical‑mathematical system where “monomers” are typed 12‑primitive tuples (Ð, Þ, Ř, Φ, ƒ, Ç, Γ, ɢ, ⊙, Ħ, Σ, Ω). Tools like `close`, `phase_reconstruct`, `forge`, `material`, `containment_boundary`, `cl9nk`, `imasm` let you chain, cyclise, check spectral purity (ρ = spectral radius, 2 ⇒ pure cycle), verify floor containment (SIXTEEN_3 ∧ CLINK‑L8), and promote to CLINK L9.
